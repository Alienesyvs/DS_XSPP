Türkçe Metin

Motorlu el aleti incelemesi sırasında plastik kısımda çatlak gözlendi. Satın alma sonrasında benzer sorunlar başka müşteriler tarafından da bildirildiğinden sorunun kullanıcı hatasından kaynaklanmadığı belirlendi. Yapılan incelemede el aletinde herhangi bir darbe veya düşme izine rastlanmadı. Servis kılavuzuna göre 6752401'i değiştirmeyi planlıyoruz. Herhangi bir öneriniz veya tavsiyeniz var mı?

English Text

A crack was observed in the plastic part during the motor handpiece inspection. Since similar issues were reported by other customers after purchase, it was determined that the problem was not due to user error. Upon investigation, no signs of impact or dropping were found on the handpiece. According to service manual, we're planning to replace 6752401. Do you recommend or advise anything?

English Text

The device is displaying an Apex measurement error during operation and showing the S-03009 error code. Other components of the device have been tested, and the service manual instructions have been reviewed. It has been determined that the issue is related to the (6808278) PCB. Additionally, a crack was observed in the plastic part during the motor handpiece inspection. Since similar issues were reported by other customers after purchase, it was determined that the problem was not due to user error. According to service manual, we're planning to replace 6808278 and 6752401. Do you recommend or advise anything?
